How to play?

Place your bid, for the art. You have only $1000 to spend so, spend carefully.

If you won the bid, you add your score by the price of the art.

If you lose the bid, you add your score based on how close your bid was with the sold price.

Total 9 rounds, share your bid score with your friends.
